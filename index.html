Security Inspections

1. Testing Procedures

Tensile Stitch Testing

All seams are mechanically tested under load scales to verify stitch strength, ensuring children cannot pull seams apart to access stuffing.

Torque & Component Security

Verifies that small components (plastic eyes, noses, custom labels) sustain high rotational and pulling forces, preventing choking hazards.

Drop & Structural Durability

Toys undergo simulated impact drop tests to guarantee that internal wire frames or plastic sound boxes remain safely enclosed inside the plush body.

Quality Directives

2. Safety Standards

CE Conformity

Meets all safety, health, and environmental protection requirements for products sold within the European Economic Area.

EN71 Part 1, 2 & 3 Compliance

Ensures structural safety, mechanical properties, slow flammability, and the complete absence of heavy metals in fabrics or stuffing fibers.

ASTM F963 & BIS Standard

Certified under the standard consumer safety specifications for toy safety in the United States and Bureau of Indian Standards registration in India.

Global Logistics

3. Export Compliance

FOB & CIF Packaging

Corrugated seaworthy outer cartons, individual inner polybagging, desiccant pouch inserts, and precise dimensional packaging to prevent moisture and box collapsing.

Container Clearance Support

Port Logistics Handling

Direct container shipping dispatch services from India's major ICD and port hubs (including Nhava Sheva/JNPT) directly to your destination ports.
